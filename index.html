FAQ

Common questions

Does Outlook Power Tools work with New Outlook?

Yes. It works on outlook.live.com, outlook.office.com, and outlook.office365.com — all versions of New Outlook in the browser. Compatible with any Chromium-based browser.

Where are Quick Steps in New Outlook?

Microsoft limited Quick Steps in New Outlook. This extension restores them — create custom action combos (mark as read + move to folder, flag + forward) and run them with one click or a keyboard shortcut.

Is it free?

Yes. The free plan includes 3 email templates, 3 quick steps, 3 keyboard shortcuts, and the attachment reminder (always free). Pro ($3.99/month or $29.99/year) unlocks unlimited everything.

Does it read my emails?

No. Templates and shortcuts are stored locally in your browser. The extension does not read, store, or transmit your email content. See our privacy policy.
