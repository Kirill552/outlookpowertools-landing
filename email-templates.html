Email templates

Outlook email templates, canned responses, and saved replies for New Outlook.

Create reusable email templates in Outlook Web, then insert them into a new message or reply with two clicks. Templates can include a subject line, recipients, and body text.

Add to Chrome Missing features guide

Short answer

Outlook Power Tools adds reusable templates to New Outlook for support replies, follow-ups, scheduling, out-of-office messages, sales outreach, and any text you send repeatedly.

Outlook Power Tools templates are stored locally in Chrome storage. The extension does not transmit template content or email content to a remote server.

Template use cases

Use caseExample
SupportTroubleshooting steps, intake questions, status updates, refund replies.
SalesFollow-ups, meeting confirmations, pricing replies, proposal handoffs.
SchedulingMeeting proposals, reschedule notes, timezone confirmations.
OperationsApprovals, reminders, internal handoffs, repeat admin messages.

How it works

  1. Install Outlook Power Tools from the Chrome Web Store.
  2. Open Outlook Web and create or reply to an email.
  3. Save a message as a template or choose one of the built-in templates.
  4. Insert the template into compose whenever the same reply is needed.

FAQ

Can I create email templates in New Outlook?

Yes. Outlook Power Tools lets you save reusable replies with subject lines, recipients, and body text.

What is the difference between templates and canned responses?

Templates are complete reusable messages. Canned responses are shorter saved replies for common questions and support workflows.

Are built-in templates included?

Yes. Built-in templates cover acknowledgments, follow-ups, scheduling, out-of-office messages, and support replies.
