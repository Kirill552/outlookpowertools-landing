Short answer

Outlook Power Tools is a free Outlook ad blocker for Chrome. It hides Outlook Web ad surfaces while keeping email templates, Quick Steps, PDF export, shortcuts, unread badge, and attachment reminder in the same extension.

Outlook Power Tools ad blocking runs in the browser on outlook.live.com, outlook.office.com, and outlook.office365.com. The extension does not sell ad inventory or replace Outlook ads with its own ads.
